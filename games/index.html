Why games?

The first consol I ever played was a Sega Dreamcast. It intaduced me to gaming and it's been something that kept my attention and intrest ever since. Playing video games feels like creating your own story rather then watching it play out. It's also extremly fun!

Favorites

  • Frogger 2 Swampys Revenge

    This is more then just the typical Frogger game where your try to cross a street and not get hit by a car. This is an adventure. Featuring a story mode where you play as multiple frogs to rescue your frog children. One of the first games Iever played and enjoyed.

  • Modern Warfare 2

    A Call of Duty game does not need too much explaining. It is a first person shooter. However, it is my favorite of the genre. Featuring a captivating single player campaign and an enjoyabe muliplayer experience.

  • Witcher 3 Wild Hunt

    Based on a book this is the third and final instalment of the series. Winning Game of the Year this game has a great story,side story, and game play in general. It's an adventure game where you play as Geralt, a Witcher, and slay monsters for profit and to save your friends.
